# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document specifies requirements for upgrading all JARVIS AI systems and buttons to Master Level - making them extremely powerful, advanced, and capable of handling any task at expert level. The system will enhance all existing AI capabilities, add new master-level features, and create advanced powerful buttons that can execute complex operations with a single click.

## Glossary

- **Master_Level_AI**: AI systems operating at expert/master level with maximum capabilities
- **Power_Multiplier**: System that multiplies AI power and effectiveness
- **Advanced_Button_System**: Powerful buttons that execute complex multi-step operations
- **AI_Enhancer**: Component that upgrades existing AI capabilities
- **Master_Brain**: Ultra-powerful central AI brain coordinating all systems
- **Quantum_Processor**: Advanced processing system for maximum speed and power
- **Neural_Amplifier**: System that amplifies AI neural network capabilities
- **Super_Automation**: Advanced automation that handles complex tasks automatically
- **Intelligence_Booster**: Component that boosts AI intelligence and reasoning
- **Power_Button**: Single button that executes powerful complex operations
