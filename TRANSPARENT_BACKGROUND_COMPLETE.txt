🎨 TECHNICAL DETAILS (Technical বিবরণ)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Renderer Settings:
   • alpha: true (transparency enabled)
   • preserveDrawingBuffer: true (for screenshots)
   • setClearColor(0x000000, 0) (fully transparent)

Scene Settings:
   • scene.background = null (no background)
   • No fog (for clean transparency)

Material Settings:
   • Skin: opaque (not transparent)
   • Eyes: emissive glow
   • Glow effects: transparent with opacity

Export Settings:
   • Format: GLB (binary)
   • Transparency: preserved
   • Compatible: Three.js, Unity, Unreal, Babylon.js
